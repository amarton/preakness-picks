Race 1: 10:30am

Finnegan ships in from Keeneland, where he finished a tidy second in better company. La Nina and Sunshine Life also ship in from slightly classi- er racing circuits, and thus should improve off recent efforts.

Sunshine Life finished third in a field of four at Aqueduct, but the class of that maiden race was better than the group she faces Saturday. Hard to say what she’s got, but at 5-1, it’s a decent value in an otherwise even field.

Take Finnegan to win. He looks to be the best. Wheel Finnegan with ALL in exacta wagers.
